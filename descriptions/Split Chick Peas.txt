It boasts an impressive nutritional profile, providing an abundance of essential vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber. With its high protein content, it contributes to building and repairing tissues, supporting muscle growth, and promoting satiety. Its wealth of dietary fiber aids digestion, regulates bowel movements, and promotes a feeling of fullness, contributing to weight management. Iron, a vital component of red blood cells, is abundantly present, aiding in oxygen transport and preventing anemia. Phosphorus, crucial for strong bones and teeth, is also found in ample quantities. The presence of potassium supports healthy blood pressure levels and electrolyte balance. Moreover, it's a rich source of folate, essential for DNA synthesis and red blood cell formation, making it beneficial for pregnant women and individuals looking to conceive. Zinc, a trace mineral involved in immune function and wound healing, is another notable nutrient found in split chickpeas. Furthermore, its low glycemic index makes it suitable for individuals with diabetes or those seeking to stabilize blood sugar levels.
