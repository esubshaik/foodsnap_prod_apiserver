It offers a remarkable nutritional composition, boasting a diverse range of essential vitamins, minerals, and beneficial compounds. Jackfruit is a powerhouse of vitamin C, providing nearly 10% of the daily recommended intake, supporting a robust immune system and promoting overall health. It also boasts significant levels of potassium, aiding in maintaining healthy blood pressure and promoting cardiovascular well-being. Moreover, its abundance of dietary fiber aids digestion, promotes a feeling of fullness, and supports a healthy gut microbiome. Jackfruit is a remarkable source of antioxidants, notably flavonoids and lignans, renowned for their ability to combat free radical damage and potentially reduce the risk of chronic diseases. Furthermore, it contains a unique enzyme, bromelain, which possesses anti-inflammatory properties, potentially alleviating muscle soreness and inflammation. Jackfruit is a good source of vitamin A, essential for maintaining healthy vision, skin, and immune function. It also provides a notable quantity of magnesium, which contributes to muscle relaxation, nerve function, and energy metabolism. Additionally, it contains significant amounts of copper, a vital nutrient involved in various physiological processes, including energy production and immune system function. Furthermore, jackfruit is a good source of riboflavin (vitamin B2), which plays a crucial role in energy metabolism, cell growth, and red blood cell production. It is also a good source of vitamin K, essential for proper blood clotting and bone health. Moreover, jackfruit contains a wide array of phytonutrients, including carotenoids, flavonoids, and saponins, which possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and potential anticancer properties.
